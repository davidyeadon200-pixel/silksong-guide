Main Characters

Hornet

The main protagonist of Hollow Knight: Silksong. After capture and taken to Pharloom, she escapes and must ascend to the top of the citadel.

The Knight

The protagonist of Hollow Knight. A discarded vessel born in the Abyss, carrying Void within its shell.

❮

Act I – The Beginning

❯

Moss Mother

The first boss encounter in Silksong. Found in the Moss Grotto.

Bell Beast

A major NPC boss who becomes the fast‑travel mechanic after befriending him. Found in The Marrow.

Lace

A fencer created from silk, harboring deep resentment. Found in the Deep Docks.

Fourth Chorus

A colossal rusted automaton powered by silkflies. Found in the Far Fields.

❮

Act II – Citadel

❯

Cogwork Dancers

Twin automaton bosses powered by the soul of a Verdian prince. Found in Cogwork Core.

Lace (2nd Encounter)

A three‑phase boss fight acting as the gateway to the end of Act II. Found in The Cradle.

Grand Mother Silk

The primary antagonist of Silksong, a powerful Higher Being. Found at the top of the Citadel.

❮

Act III – Abyss

❯

Shrine Guardian Seth

A fast‑paced armored ant warrior guarding the Grand Gate. Found in Shellwood.

Nyleth

A plant‑like ancient being fought in the Heart of the Woods quest. Found in Shellwood.

Curst King Khann

A mighty general of the Order of Karak. Found in the Sands of Karak.

Skarrsinger Karmelita

The former song‑queen of the Skarr, known for her operatic voice. Found in Far Fields.

Lost Lace

A darker version of Lace, guardian of the Abyss of Pharloom. Found in The Abyss
