Mechanics

Healing with Silk

Hornet heals by weaving silk into a Bind, restoring health in a single, fast animation. It costs full silk segments, making resource management more tactical than in Hollow Knight.

Silk Gauge

Defeating enemies and striking foes fills Hornet’s silk spool. Silk fuels healing, abilities, and special attacks, acting as the core resource of the entire combat system.

Needle Combat

Hornet fights with her needle, giving her longer reach, faster strikes, and more mobility-based combos. Combat is more aggressive and rewards precision and timing.

Tools

Tools are reusable items that consume silk to unleash powerful effects like bombs, traps, or mobility boosts. They function like customizable abilities that shape your playstyle.

Crafting

Hornet can gather materials from enemies and the environment to craft new tools or upgrade existing ones. This adds progression depth and encourages exploration.

Movement & Agility

Hornet is far more agile than the Knight, with faster dashes, wall-climbs, and aerial control. The game emphasizes speed, fluidity, and acrobatic traversal.

Quests

NPCs give structured quests that track objectives, rewards, and story progress. This replaces Hollow Knight’s looser NPC interactions with a more guided system.

Enemy Hunts

Some quests involve tracking specific enemies across multiple areas. These hunts blend exploration and combat, rewarding players with materials or upgrades.

Checkpoints

Instead of benches, Hornet uses rest points that save progress and allow tool management. They are placed to support faster, more dynamic gameplay.

Maps & Cartography

Hornet collects maps from Shakra and updates them by finding checkpoints. Mapping is streamlined to match the game’s faster pace.

Crests

Silksong replaces charms with crests, which modify abilities, movement, or combat. Crests offer build variety but are balanced around Hornet’s higher mobility.

Enemy Aggression

Enemies in Pharloom are faster, more varied, and often use silk-based attacks. Combat pushes the player to stay mobile rather than tank hits.

Boss Fights

Bosses emphasize multi-phase movement, verticality, and rapid attacks. Hornet’s agility is tested through pattern recognition and quick repositioning.

Ascension System

Some areas and challenges are tied to Pharloom’s theme of ascension, offering harder versions of encounters for greater rewards. This acts as optional endgame difficulty.
