Lore

Hornet’s Capture

Hornet is ambushed by silk-wrapped enemies and taken in a cage to the distant kingdom of Pharloom. Her capture is deliberate, suggesting someone there specifically needs her.

The Kingdom of Pharloom

Pharloom is a remote kingdom built on silk, song, and religious ascension. Its culture revolves around pilgrimage and climbing the great Citadel.

The Silk Queen

The Silk Queen commands the Weaves, tying her directly to Hornet’s abductions. Her motives remain unclear but are central to the kingdom’s rituals.

Pilgrims and the Tower

Bugs from across the land travel to Pharloom seeking healing or ascension through the Citadel’s trials. Hornet, unlike them, is forced into this journey.

The Role of Silk

Silk is a life-giving, binding, and restorative force that replaces Soul both mechanically and thematically. It connects Hornet, the Weavers, and Pharloom’s rituals.

Hornet’s Identity

Hornet’s royal and Weaver heritage makes her uniquely important in Pharloom. Her lineage positions her as either a threat, a tool, or a candidate for ascension.

Echoes of Infection

Pharloom shows signs of its own corruption or sickness. This mirrors Hallownest’s downfall and suggests a recurring cycle in kingdoms.

The Power of Song

Song is a spiritual and sometimes healing force used by Pharloom’s inhabitants. It acts as a counterpart to silk, shaping the kingdom’s culture and magic.

The Citadel

The Citadel is the ultimate destination of Hornet’s journey and the heart of Pharloom’s ascension rituals. Whatever purpose she was brought for awaits her at its peak.
